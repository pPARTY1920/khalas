Frequently asked questions

Once your order is confirmed, you'll see a live map on the order screen showing your rider's location. You'll also receive push notifications at each stage — confirmed, preparing, picked up, and delivered.
You can cancel within 2 minutes of placing your order. After the restaurant starts preparing your food, cancellation is no longer available. Go to My Orders → select the order → Cancel Order.
We're sorry about that! Open the order in the app, tap "Report a problem", and select "Missing item". We'll issue a credit or refund within 24 hours, no questions asked.
Zesto credits are applied instantly. Refunds to a card or mobile money typically take 3–5 business days depending on your bank or wallet provider.
At checkout, scroll down to the "Promo code" field, enter your code, and tap Apply. The discount will be shown in your order summary before you confirm payment.

Address changes are only possible before the restaurant accepts your order. Tap My Orders → select your order → Edit Address. If that option is greyed out, contact support immediately via live chat.
Go to Profile → Settings → Privacy → Delete Account. You'll be asked to confirm. Note that this action is permanent and all your order history and credits will be removed.
