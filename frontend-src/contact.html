FAQ & Quick help
Common questions
Find instant answers or pick a topic to reach the right team.
How long does it take to get a response?
Our team typically responds within 2 hours during business hours (Mon–Sat, 8am–9pm EAT). For urgent order issues, live chat is the fastest option.
My order was wrong or missing items. What do I do?
Contact us via the form above or live chat with your order number. We'll review and process a refund or re-delivery within 24 hours.
How do I get a refund?
Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method. Mobile money refunds usually arrive within 24 hours. Contact us within 24 hours of your delivery.
How do I list my restaurant on Zesto?
Fill in the contact form above and select "Partner with Zesto". Our partnerships team will reach out within 24 hours with onboarding details.
Can I become a Zesto delivery rider?
Yes! You'll need a motorcycle or bicycle, a valid ID, and a smartphone. Click "Become a rider" below or contact us selecting that topic in the form.
What cities does Zesto currently serve?
Zesto currently operates in Kampala, Abidjan, and Accra. We're expanding rapidly — sign up to be the first to know when we launch in your city!
