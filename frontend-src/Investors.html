The opportunity
Why Africa, why now
A $40B market with
no clear winner yet
Africa's food delivery market is at the same inflection point that Southeast Asia was in 2015. The fundamentals are converging — rising smartphone penetration, a growing middle class, and the rapid adoption of mobile payments.
Unlike Western markets, Africa's delivery landscape is fragmented, underserved, and ripe for a mobile-first, locally-adapted platform. Zesto was built from the ground up for this context.
📱
600M+ smartphone users by 2027
Africa has the world's fastest-growing mobile internet adoption. Our mobile-first platform captures this wave directly.
💳
Mobile money as payment rail
MTN Mobile Money, M-Pesa, and Orange Money give us access to millions of unbanked consumers traditional platforms miss.
🏙️
Rapid urbanisation
17 of the world's 30 fastest-growing cities are in Africa. Dense urban populations are the perfect environment for on-demand delivery.
🍽️
Informal restaurant sector is massive
90% of Africa's food sector is informal. We're the first platform built to onboard and digitise these restaurants at scale.
