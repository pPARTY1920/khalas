Refunds & Cancellations

You may cancel an order free of charge within 2 minutes of placing it, before the restaurant has confirmed and started preparing your order. After that window, cancellations may not be possible or may incur a fee.

You may be eligible for a refund if:

  • Your order was significantly incorrect (wrong items, missing items).
  • Your order was never delivered and you reported it within 24 hours.

Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days to the original payment method. Mobile money refunds are typically processed within 24 hours. To request a refund, contact us at support@zesto.app or via live chat within 24 hours of delivery.
