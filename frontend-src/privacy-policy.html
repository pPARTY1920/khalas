1

Introduction

Zesto Technologies Inc. ("Zesto", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use the Zesto app, website, or any of our services.

By creating an account or placing an order, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

We never sell your personal data to third parties for their own marketing purposes. Full stop.

2

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, data generated by your use of our services, and information from third parties where you've granted permission.

  • Account data: name, email address, phone number, and profile photo.
  • Delivery data: saved addresses, delivery notes, and GPS location (only when the app is in use).
  • Payment data: tokenised card details or mobile money references — we never store raw card numbers.
  • Order history: items ordered, restaurants visited, delivery times, and feedback ratings.
  • Device data: device model, OS version, app version, and crash logs used for debugging.
  • Communications: messages sent to our support team and responses we provide.
3

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Zesto's services. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders and communicate order status.
  • Connect you with restaurants and delivery riders in your area.
  • Personalise your app experience and surface relevant recommendations.
  • Send transactional notifications (order confirmed, rider on the way, delivered).
  • Detect and prevent fraud, chargebacks, and policy violations.
  • Comply with applicable laws and respond to lawful requests.

We will only send you marketing communications if you've explicitly opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time from your account settings or via the unsubscribe link in any email.

4

Sharing Your Data

We share your data only with parties essential to providing the service:

  • Restaurants: receive your first name, order details, and delivery address to prepare and fulfil your order.
  • Riders: receive your delivery address and phone number (masked) while the order is active.
  • Payment processors: securely handle payment authorisation under PCI-DSS standards.
  • Cloud infrastructure providers: host our data in encrypted, access-controlled environments.
  • Analytics tools: anonymised, aggregated usage data to help us improve the product.
5

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and understand aggregate traffic patterns. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function correctly.

6

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active, and for up to 7 years afterward for legal and tax compliance purposes. Order history is retained for 5 years. Device logs and crash data are deleted after 90 days.

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Your Rights below). Some data may be retained beyond deletion requests where required by law.

7

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated information.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Port your data to another service in a machine-readable format.
  • Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@zesto.com or use the "Data & Privacy" section in your account settings.

8

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption in transit, AES-256 encryption at rest, role-based access controls, and regular third-party security audits. No system is 100% secure; in the event of a breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law.

9

Children's Privacy

Zesto is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please reach out:

  • Email: privacy@zesto.com
  • Address: Zesto Technologies Inc., Plot 15, Kampala Road, Kampala, Uganda
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@zesto.com

We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 72 hours.
